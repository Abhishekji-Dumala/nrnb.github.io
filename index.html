National Resource for Network Biology

We are developing freely available, open source software technologies to enable network-based visualization, analysis, and biomedical discovery. We are driving research and development projects, coordinating training opportunities, and actively seeking new collaborations to develop NRNB tools and apply them to biomedical research.

Research Highlights

We can collaborate on adapting NRNB tools for use in your unique projects. If you are already using our supported tools in novel and important research, then we should be collaborating.

Upcoming Training & Events

We can establish pipelines from your databases and data warehouses to make your data more accessible and more useful. There are many opportunities to interface with NRNB tools.

The National Resource for Network Biology is an NIH Division of Biomedical Technology, Bioinformatics, and Computational Biology (BBCB) Biomedical Technology Research Center (BTRC). NRNB is funded by grant GM103504.

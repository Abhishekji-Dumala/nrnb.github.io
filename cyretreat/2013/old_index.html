10th Annual Network Biology Symposium and Cytoscape Workshop - Institut Pasteur - Paris, France

The annual Network Biology Symposium and Cytoscape Workshop brings together the entire Network Biology and Cytoscape communities, including users and developers, for an overview of network biology, and a series of tutorials, workshops, meetings and demos. The meeting kicks off with a public symposium on the latest developments in Network Biology. Please find program and registration details below.

Register Now (DEADLINE: 23 September 2013)

Also register to present a poster or Flash Talk about Network Biology or about aCytoscape App you would like to share.


Program

Wednesday, October 9th
9:00-5:00 Public Symposium on Network Biology
The symposium will focus on new developments of network-related tools and resources and their application of network analysis and visualization to biology and medicine.
Confirmed speakers so far:
  • Patrick Aloy (IRB Barcelona)
  • Nils Blüthgen (Charité Berlin)
  • Trey Ideker (UC San Diego)
  • Julio Saez-Rodriguez (Cambridge)
  • Esti Yeger-Lotem (Ben-Gurion University, Negev)
  • Hidde de Jong (INRIA Grenoble)
  • Andreas Beyer (Univ. Cologne)
  • Gary Bader (Univ. Toronto)

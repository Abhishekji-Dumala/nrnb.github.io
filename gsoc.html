NRNB Academy Summer Session

After 8 consecutive years with Google Summer of Code, they're giving us a year off to let in some new orgs and spread the love. We'll be back next year, ready to apply as a veteran mentoring organization once again. But, this year... we are running our own summer program built upon our experience with GSoC and year-round NRNB Academy programs.

NRNB Academy Summer Session will run in parallel with GSoC this summer. Though completely independent, our program will follow the same evaluation and student payment disbursement schedule. To make this happen we are launching a fundraising campaign to sponsor as many top-notch students as we can. The success of our Summer Session depends on dedicated mentors, great project ideas, amazing students, and effective fundraising. Please contact us with ideas or leads on potential corporate sponsors or use the PayPal widget below to make your own tax deductible donation. All funds will go toward student stipends for NRNB Academy Summer Session.

We are once again pooling the efforts of our colleagues and collaborators for this year's summer program. The National Resource for Network Biology (NRNB) is organizing the joint efforts of Cytoscape, WikiPathways and other network biology tools. This is a great opportunity to work at the intersection of biology and computing! We take mentoring seriously and are proud of our >90% success rate with former students and projects. But don't take our word for it, read testimonials from prior NRNB students students and mentors.

The aim of the NRNB is to advance the new science of biological networks through analytic tools, visualizations, databases and computing resources. Our technologies enable researchers to assemble and analyze these networks and to use them to better understand biological systems and, in particular, how they fail in disease. Find out more about the various software projects being developed in coordination with NRNB. And check out our latest project ideas in the tab below.
